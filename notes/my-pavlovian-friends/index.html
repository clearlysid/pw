I feel a mild flash of annoyance everytime an iPhone "dings" and, like contined robots, without fail, every single one of my friends picks up their iPhone to stare into it and see if they got a notification.

It doesn't matter whose phone was the source of the ding. Everyone's brain is just wired to pick up their phone anyways because "what if?"

No matter the task at hand: was involved in a tech discussion? watching a movie? in the middle of deciding what to have for dinner?
